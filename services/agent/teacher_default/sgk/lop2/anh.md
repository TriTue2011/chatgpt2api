# Tiếng Anh lớp 2 (gợi ý dạy)

What is your name? How are you? I am fine, thank you.

eleven đến twenty. Đếm đồ chơi.

cat, dog, bird, fish, chicken. It is a cat.

a red bag, a blue pen.
