# Tiếng Anh lớp 3 (gợi ý dạy)

## Hiện tại đơn giản
I like apples. She likes milk. He does not like...

rice, bread, milk, water, chicken, fish. I eat rice.

teacher, student, classroom, school.

sunny, rainy, hot, cold. It is sunny today.
